    • Taz

    Taz is a tool for analyzing and reporting on your Tezos transactions.

    Taz does three things to help analyze your Tezos transactions.

    1. Collect. It downloads transactions and other data needed for processing.
    2. Classify. It analyzes and classifies your transactions within and across accounts.
    3. Collate. It gathers, sorts, and further classifies transactions.

    From these three things, Taz is also able to help automate creating reports like income statements and gains and losses reports.

    At this time, Taz does not automatically download your data from centralized exchanges. You must manually create a CSV of any activity that happens outside of the Tezos blockchain.

    Creating your exchange CSV

    At the moment, you need to manually create the CSV for any exchange transactions. You can find a template/example HERE

    Headers

    Include these headers in your CSV.

    • timestamp
    • type
    • fiat
    • in_amt
    • in_token
    • in_token_id
    • in_token_from
    • out_amt
    • out_token
    • out_token_id
    • out_token_to
    • fees
    • account
    • op

    Operation hashes

    For outgoing transfer types, make sure to include the operation hash in the op column so that it can be matched correctly with the corresponding blockchain record.

    Fiat

    The fiat column is used to calculate cost basis. Here is how to set it, based on type:

    • receive: price per coin for in_token
    • interest: price per coin for in_token
    • trade: price per coin for out_token
    • not needed for other types
